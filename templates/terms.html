2. Key Definitions

Client

You! A registered user who requests and pays for errand services through the Platform.

Runner

An independent contractor registered on ErrandGo who completes errands for Clients.

Errand

A task, chore, or delivery requested by a Client and completed by a Runner.

Service Agreement

The agreement between you and a Runner for a specific errand.

Platform Fee

Our service charge for providing the matching platform. Free for clients

Our 1-5 star rating system for Clients and Runners to rate each other.

5. Posting Errands

How to Post an Errand

1

Clear Description

Describe exactly what you need done. Be specific about requirements, quantities, and preferences.

2

Set Budget

Specify how much you're willing to pay. You can also mark it as "Negotiable."

3

Location Details

Provide accurate pickup and delivery addresses with any special instructions.

4

Timeline

Specify when you need the errand completed (ASAP, specific time, or deadline).

Illegal activities or items
Prescription drugs or medications
Alcohol for minors
Surveillance or spying
Violent or dangerous tasks
Relationship-related tasks (like spying on partners)

Use Photos

Add photos to show exactly what you need

Be Realistic

Give reasonable deadlines

Communicate

Respond promptly to Runner questions

Fair Payment

Offer competitive rates for good service
