Runner Guidelines

How ErrandGo Works for Runners

Follow these steps and rules to earn safely and keep a high rating.

🔄 Step‑by‑Step Process

1

Accept an Errand

When a new job appears, review the details and tap Accept. You can also enable Auto‑Accept in Settings.

2

Share Your Location

The sender may request your live location. Keep Location Services on to allow them to track the delivery.

3

Pick Up & Deliver

Collect the item (or purchase it if needed – see below). Deliver within the agreed time and always handle packages with care.

4

Mark as Delivered

Once the client receives the item, tap Mark as Completed in the app. The client will then confirm receipt.

💰 Payments & Purchases

Buying Items on Behalf of Client

If the errand requires buying goods, use your own money. The total (cost of goods + your service fee) will be reimbursed upon delivery. Keep the receipt!

Getting Paid

The client pays the full amount (item price + delivery fee) when you hand over the package. If the client refuses to pay, do not argue. Report the issue immediately using the app.

ErrandGo Protects You

If you report a non‑paying client, ErrandGo will pay you the full amount you are owed within 24 hours. You never lose your money.

⚠️ Important Rules & Tips

✅
Always confirm the errand details (pickup, drop‑off, special requests) before starting.
📱
Use in‑app chat for all communication – never share personal numbers.
🚫
Never open or tamper with sealed packages.
🕒
Notify the sender immediately if you’re delayed.
💬
Stay polite – even in difficult situations. Report problems, don’t confront.
⭐
High ratings lead to more job offers and free errand bonuses!

🤝 Client Confirmation

After you mark the errand as Completed, the client will receive a notification to confirm delivery. Both confirmations ensure the job is closed and payment is released.

By following these guidelines you help keep ErrandGo safe and reliable for everyone.
